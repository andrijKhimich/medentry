The MedEntry Foundation - Giving Back

Since 2002, The MedEntry Foundation has been dedicated to its mission: providing access to quality education for Australians limited by low socio-economic conditions and students in rural areas.

We believe the quality of medical care in Australia will be greatly improved by the inclusion and development of the talented students who have benefited from our programs, scholarships and donations.

Committed to communities

$1.4 Million contributed in scholarships and bursaries to 3,000 disadvantaged students.

Free UCAT preparation for recipients of rural workforce agency grants.

$200,000 in donations to not-for-profit organisations including The Flying Doctors, Red Cross and UNICEF.

Rural Agency Partnerships

MedEntry has a commitment to improving access to education for students from underserviced areas, particularly rural communities.

Our partnerships with government-funded rural workforce agencies aim to increase the health workforce in such communities. We’ve enjoyed long associations with the RDWA (Rural Doctors Workforce Agency) and RWAV (Rural Workforce Association Victoria).

MedEntry provides UCAT preparation free of charge for rural students who receive grants from these organisations (please contact the relevant agency for details).

Scholarships and Bursaries

MedEntry currently offers 17 scholarships, bursaries and discounts to students, especially those from low socio-economic backgrounds and those residing in rural or remote areas.

More than 3,000 disadvantaged students are now qualified medical practitioners thanks to the $1.4 million in scholarships and bursaries contributed by The MedEntry Foundation.

Charity Partnership

With a focus on health and education, The MedEntry Foundation partners with a different charity organisation each year. In total, we’ve donated $200,000 to more than 12 organisations supporting disadvantaged Australians.

MedEntry is proud to have been associated with some of Australia’s most respected not-for-profit organisations.

MedEntry Alumni

MedEntry’s commitment to students doesn’t end after admission to medical school.

The MedEntry Alumni provides ongoing support including exclusive resources, mentorships and discounts:

  • Resources to assist those at medical school and in postgraduate training programs;
  • Advice from current senior medical students, interns, hospital medical officers, registrars and consultants ;
  • Mentorship from successful practicing doctors;
  • Discounts for MedEntry services including postgraduate interview training;
  • Discounts for family members.
