Why choose Medentry?

The UCAT exam is the largest obstacle between students and a career in medicine. That’s why choosing the right UCAT preparation provider is critical. MedEntry helps more students pass (and excel) than any other UCAT organisation.

Unrivalled Results

Every year, MedEntry students achieve the highest scores in the UCAT. 92% of students who complete our full program are offered interviews, and 88% are offered places in medical schools.

Of the thousands of people studying medicine in Australia right now, most are MedEntry students.

Real doctors providing expert guidance

For more than 20 years, our team of practising doctors and academics has helped 20,000 students realise their dream of becoming medical practitioners.

Our lecturers have the deepest understanding of medical entry, including crucial inside information.

Australia’s most acclaimed UCAT prep

Getting into medicine is hard. Really hard. We provide the tools, tuition and personal guidance that help our students realise their dream of becoming medical practitioners.

MedEntry has received more than 3,000 five-star reviews and an average rating of 4.9 out of 5. When it comes to student feedback, no other UCAT organisation comes close.

A Dedicated Team

Medentry Team

20 years ago, as MedEntry founders Dr Ray Boyapati and Dr Ann Deely prepared for their medical entrance examinations, they were shocked by the lack of quality resources available to students.

Since then, Ray and Ann have become highly awarded specialists (in Gastroenterology and Dermatology respectively). They’ve also created Australia’s leading UCAT organisation, helping 20,000 students pass their UCAT exams and launch their careers in medicine.
